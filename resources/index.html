Research Reports

While this website does not include an analysis of affordable housing in San Francisco, we’ve included links to reports that have:

  • “All Cities Are Not Created Unequal,” Brookings Institution, February 2014. Article online. While not explicitly about affordable housing, this article links housing affordability with income inequality.
  • “Analysis of Tenant Displacement in San Francisco,” Budget and Legislative Analyst, October 2013. Full report in PDF.
  • “Housing Element of the General Plan,” City Planning, 2014 update. 3 part report available online.
  • “Housing Work Group 2014: Findings and Recommendations,” City and County of San Francisco, Office of the Mayor. Full report in PDF.
  • “How California’s Housing Market is Failing To Meet the Needs of Low Income Families”, California Housing Partnership Corporation, February 2014. Full report in PDF.
  • “How San Francisco County’s Housing Market is Failing to Meet the Needs of Low-Income Families,” California Housing Partnership Corporation, February 2014. Full report in PDF.
  • “Performance Audit of the San Francisco’s Affordable Housing Policies and Programs,” Budget and Legislative Analyst, January 2012. Full report in PDF.
  • “Performance Audit of the San Francisco Housing Authority,” Budget and Legislative Analyst, June 2013. Full report in PDF.
  • “San Francisco's Affordable Housing Affordability Gap,” Mayor’s Office of Housing and Community Development. Full powerpoint in PDF.
  • ”SFHA Re-Envisioning: Recommendations to Mayor Ed Lee on how to transform the San Francisco Housing Authority,” Mayor’s Office of Housing and Community Development. Full report in PDF.
  • “The Mayor’s Office of Housing: Under Pressure and Challenged to Preserve Diversity,” Civil Grand Jury June 2014. Full report in PDF.
